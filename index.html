Rules

Teams :

Should have minimum of 11 members (Excluding Team Captain and Team Mentor).

Should have at least 1 girl.

Players need for each games :

Cricket - 8 players

Volley ball - 6 players

Carrom board - min of 3 players

TT - min of 3 players

Shuttle - min of 3 players

Note :

One can participate in multiple games

It is a mixed-match tournament.

Must do :

Girls must participate-in at least one of their interested games.

Auction points :

You can find players in auction with their starting points in Auction List tab of this pages.

Maximum points for Team is 15000. Each team captains and mentors should use it efficiently to select their team.

Team Captains and Team Mentors should consider all games before selecting a player. So that you can form a balanced team in all games.
